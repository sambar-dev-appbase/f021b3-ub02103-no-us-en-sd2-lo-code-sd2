What's the difference?

Who is an analyst? An analyst does market research, studies market conditions, evaluates past and present data and from this analysis makes buying or selling recommendations for investors.

Who is a user? A user is an individual investor and does market trading based on the ideas/suggestions provided on this platform by the analysts for his or her personal account.
